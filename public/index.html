Pro Feature

Your lyrics, written in light

MusicParticle Pro detects what song is playing and renders the lyrics as part of the simulation itself. Not overlaid text. Actual particles.

Particles peel away from the simulation shape and form the words of each lyric line.

When the next line arrives, the letters scatter back into the geometry, then reform as new words.

Song detection happens automatically. If the room goes silent, it re-listens and picks up the next track.
