Pro

Lyrics formed from particles

The app identifies what song is playing, finds synced lyrics, and pulls particles out of the simulation to form the words being sung. Each line scatters and reforms. During instrumental breaks, the particles return to the shape. Text inherits the visual style and color of the active simulation.
